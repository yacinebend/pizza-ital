About Us


Welcome to Maestro Pizzaiolo where we bring authentic taste of Italy to your plate.
Located in the heart of London N8 7NU, we are dedicated to providing our customers with highest quality of food and service.
At Maestro Pizzaiolo, our team of chefs are passionate about crafting the finest handmade pizzas using only the freshest, locally sourced ingredients.
Enjoy a variety of other Italian dishes, including homemade pastas, salads, and desserts prepared right before your eyes.

A warm and inviting atmosphere just for you where you can relax and enjoy your meal with friends and family.

Our attentive staff always ensure that your every need is taken care of.
Whether you're stopping by for a quick lunch, a business meeting or a romantic dinner, your visit to Maestro Pizzaiolo will truly be an unforgettable one.
